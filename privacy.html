Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Welcome to the Sudarshan Fitness application and member portal. Your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, protect, and use your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

To provide high-quality gym management and console services, we collect the following types of information when you register or use the portal:

  • Personal Identity: Full Name, Gender, Date of Birth.
  • Contact Details: Mobile Number, Email Address.
  • Membership & Subscription: Package plan selection, payment status, active dates, and check-in history.
  • Photos: Profile photos uploaded for membership verification and visitor security.
  • Biometric Data Note: All fingerprint and scanner templates are stored locally on the physical biometric machines in the gym and are never transmitted to our online web servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is utilized strictly for the following operational tasks:

  • Verifying and managing gym membership accounts.
  • Tracking visitor management and security logs.
  • Sending membership receipts and updates via WhatsApp/Email.
  • Processing gym check-ins and session capacity management.

3. Data Protection and Security

We deploy robust security measures to protect your personal details:

  • All online data transmission is encrypted using Secure Socket Layer (SSL/HTTPS) technology.
  • Data is stored on secure, access-controlled databases with strict firewall rules.
  • We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

4. Your Consent

By using the Sudarshan Fitness portal or application, you consent to our Privacy Policy.
